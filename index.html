    Accessibility Statement

    Last updated: January 1, 2026

    Our Commitment

    Imani's Beauty is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We believe beauty is for every queen — and that includes making sure everyone can browse and shop with ease.

    Standards We Follow

    We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA, covering a wide range of disabilities including visual, auditory, physical, cognitive, and neurological.

    What We've Done

    • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard.
    • Alt Text: Images include descriptive alternative text for screen readers.
    • Colour Contrast: We maintain sufficient contrast ratios for readability.
    • Readable Fonts: Clear, legible typefaces at appropriate sizes throughout.
    • Responsive Design: Fully responsive across all devices and screen sizes.
    • Descriptive Links: Links are written to be meaningful out of context.

    Assistive Technologies

    Our site is designed to be compatible with screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver), screen magnification software, speech recognition tools, and keyboard-only navigation.

    Known Limitations

    While we strive for full accessibility, some areas may not yet meet all WCAG 2.1 AA criteria. We are actively working to identify and address these gaps.

    Feedback & Contact

    If you experience any difficulty accessing any part of our site, please contact us — we aim to respond within 2 business days.
